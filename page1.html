I am a fourth year student at the National Law School of India University, Bangalore (NLSIU), with a keen interest in information technology law. I was the recipient of the International Technology Law Association’s (ITechLaw) Student Manuscript Award 2018 for my paper on regulatory sandboxes in the Indian context and pursuant to the same, presented my paper at the ITechLaw India Conference 2018. I have served as an Invited Member of the Karnataka IT Ministry's Consultation Team on Innovation and Regulatory Sandboxes where I co-authored the Karnataka Innovation Authority Bill, 2018.

I currently serve as the Deputy Editor-in-Chief of the Indian Journal of Law and Technology (IJLT), India’s first and foremost journal on the interface of law and technology. I completed my ICSE at Bishop Cotton Boys' School and my A-Level at Vidyashilp Academy, following which I managed to secure All India Rank 1 in the Common Law Admission Test (CLAT).
